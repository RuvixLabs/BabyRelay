# BabyRelay App Icon Concepts - Happy Baby Face Pass

Generated with `gpt-image-2` on 2026-06-16 after Joe asked for an app icon
direction based on a close-up happy baby face.

## Concepts

1. `flat_smile` - clean flat mascot face with a simple happy expression.
2. `soft_clay_face` - rounded 3D clay mascot face.
3. `paper_cut_face` - face inside a soft paper-cut swaddle hood.
4. `peekaboo_crop` - close cropped happy face in a sage swaddle hood.
5. `moon_cheek` - happy face with a crescent moon cheek cue.
6. `minimal_round_face` - simplest round baby-face launcher mark.
7. `glow_face` - warm dawn-glow baby face on navy.
8. `swaddle_face` - happy close-up face inside a diagonal swaddle hood.

## Initial Recommendation

`06_minimal_round_face` is the cleanest pure app icon. `04_peekaboo_crop` and
`08_swaddle_face` feel warmer and more premium. `02_soft_clay_face` and
`05_moon_cheek` are cute but risk looking like a generic baby app. Overall, this
direction is emotionally clear but less specific to BabyRelay's shared-care idea
than the hand/cradle concepts.

No live app icon files were changed in `ios/Runner/Assets.xcassets`.
